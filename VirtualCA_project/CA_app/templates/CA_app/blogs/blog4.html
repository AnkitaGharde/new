CA Exams December 2021 Open Online Forms Submit Last Date

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) has initiated the Online forms of CA Foundation, Final – old, Final-new, Intermediate (IPC) & Intermediate) Exams for December 2021. 30th Sept 2021 will be the due date for submitting the forms online excluding any late fee and 3rd Oct 2021 is the due date for online form submission with late fees.The dates have been declared by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) for the next Chartered Accountants Foundation, Intermediate (IPC) (Old Scheme), Intermediate (New Scheme), Final (Old Scheme as well as New Scheme) Examinations. 13th, 15th, 17th & 19th December 2021 are the dates on which the foundation course for examination will be conducted beneath the latest scheme. 6th, 8th, 10th & 12th December 2021, are the notified dates on which the Intermediate (IPC) under Old Scheme, Group I Examination shall get conducted and on 14th, 16th & 18th December 2021 Group II Examination shall get conducted. On the dates 6th, 8th, 10th & 12th December 2021, the Intermediate (IPC) beneath the new Scheme, Group I Examination shall get conducted besides that on 14th, 16th, 18th & 20th December 2021 Group II Examination shall get conducted. On 5th, 7th, 9th & 11th December 2021, the final course examination beneath the previous Scheme along with the latest Scheme, Group I Examination will be conducted and on 13th, 15th, 17th & 19th December 2021 Group II examination shall be conducted.
